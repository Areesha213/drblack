Introduction Of Dr Ahmer

Dr. Ahmer is a highly qualified General, Hepatobiliary and Laparoscopic Surgeon, he has more than 20 years of experience in surgery. Throughout his career, Dr. Ahmer has worked as a Consultant General Surgeon in some of the world’s leading medical facilities in France, the United Kingdom, the United States of America, as well as Muscat and Pakistan. A Master’s degree in General Surgery was obtained by him in Pakistan, while another master’s degree was completed in Hepatobiliary Pancreatic surgery. Additionally, he was a Fellow in Laparoscopic Surgery from India (FMAS) and a Fellow in American College of Physicians and Surgeons (USA).

  • Fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery – (FMAS)
